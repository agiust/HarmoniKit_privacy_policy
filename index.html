HarmoniKit — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the HarmoniKit mobile application ("the App") handles information. The App is provided by Alessandro ("Service Provider"). By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data we collect

The App does not collect, transmit or store any personal data on remote servers. All data created by your use of the App stays on your device.

Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number or any other identifier;
  • Your IP address or device identifiers;
  • Your location;
  • Analytics, telemetry, crash reports or usage statistics;
  • Audio recordings (see §2 for how audio is processed locally).

2. Microphone access

The App requests the RECORD_AUDIO permission so that two on-device features can work:

  • Pitch Detector — analyses incoming audio in real time to detect the musical pitch you are playing. Audio is processed in memory and is never written to disk or transmitted.
  • Recorder — when you explicitly start a recording, audio is saved as a file in the App's private storage on your device only. Recordings are not uploaded anywhere. You can delete them at any time from within the App, or by clearing the App's data in your device settings.

You may revoke microphone permission at any time in your device's system settings; the relevant features will then be disabled.

3. Locally-stored data

The App stores the following data locally on your device using Android's standard storage APIs (SharedPreferences and the App's private cache directory):

  • Saved tabs and gig set lists you create;
  • Audio recordings you make (Recorder feature);
  • Your free-tier usage counter and the timestamp of the current 24-hour window;
  • Whether you have purchased the in-app "Unlimited Access" upgrade.

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App, or clearing the App's storage in system settings, removes all of it.

4. In-app purchases

The App offers a single optional one-time in-app purchase ("Unlimited Access") processed by Google Play Billing. The purchase flow takes place inside the Google Play Store; the Service Provider does not see, collect or store your payment details. The App only receives from Google a confirmation that the product has been purchased, and a cryptographic signature used solely to verify that the entitlement is genuine.

Google Play's own Privacy Policy applies to the payment process: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Third-party services

The App integrates with one third-party service:

  • Google Play Services / Google Play Billing Library — used solely to deliver in-app purchases. See Google's policy linked above.

The App does not use any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, attribution or social-media SDKs.

6. Children

The App is suitable for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children. If you believe a child has used the App, no action is required because no personal data is transmitted off the device.

7. Your rights

Because the App does not collect personal data, there is no remote data to access, correct, export or delete. You retain full control over all data on your device and can remove it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage.

8. Security

All App data is stored in your device's private app storage area, which Android isolates from other apps. The Service Provider takes reasonable measures to ensure that the App does not transmit user data in transit, because no such transmission takes place.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating this page and the effective date above. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
